OUR COMPANY

About PSL Group

PSL Construction Complete Sole Co., Ltd. (also known as PSL Services Sole Co., Ltd.) was established in 2018, with Mrs. Thepphalak Khamvisorn as President. The company's core objectives and scope of operations are as follows:

🏗️

Infrastructure Development

Focused on the construction of roads, bridges, airports, housing, offices, residential buildings, and factories across the country.

🛡️

Government Procurement & Technology

Experienced in procuring military equipment, developing medical and pharmaceutical technology, developing unmanned aerial vehicles (drones), and the garment industry.

🤝

Strategic Cooperation

Committed to being a complete construction conglomerate that cooperates with both domestic and international partners to implement urgent strategic projects for the Party and Government.
